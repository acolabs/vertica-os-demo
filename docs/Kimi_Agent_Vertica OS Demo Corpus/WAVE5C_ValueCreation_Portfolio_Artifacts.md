## C.3: INTERVENTION PLAYBOOK - PIPELINE RESET

### TRIGGER CONDITIONS
Pipeline Reset intervention is triggered when ANY of the following conditions are met:

| Trigger | Threshold | Data Source | Escalation Timeline |
|---------|-----------|-------------|---------------------|
| Pipeline Coverage Ratio | <2.5x for 2+ weeks | CRM | Immediate |
| Pipeline Velocity Decline | >20% QoQ | CRM/BI | 1 week |
| Win Rate Compression | >5pp decline | CRM | 2 weeks |
| Stale Opportunity Accumulation | >30% of pipeline >90 days | CRM | Immediate |
| Forecast Accuracy | <70% for 2+ months | CRM | 1 week |

---

### INTERVENTION WORKFLOW

```
PHASE 1: TRIAGE (Week 1)
├── Day 1-2: Emergency pipeline audit
│   ├── Export all open opportunities
│   ├── Calculate pipeline health score
│   └── Identify stale/dead opportunities
├── Day 3-4: Root cause analysis
│   ├── Rep-by-rep pipeline review
│   ├── Stage-by-stage conversion analysis
│   └── Market/competitive factor review
└── Day 5: Intervention plan approval
    ├── Present findings to CEO

    └── Lock intervention resources

PHASE 2: RESET (Week 2-3)
├── Week 2: Pipeline Surgery
│   ├── Remove confirmed dead opportunities
│   ├── Re-stage remaining opportunities
│   ├── Update close dates (realistic)
│   └── Re-assign misaligned opportunities
└── Week 3: Rebuild Foundation
    ├── Refresh ICP definition
    ├── Update qualification criteria
    ├── Deploy new pipeline stages (if needed)
    └── Train team on reset standards

PHASE 3: STABILIZE (Week 4-6)
├── Week 4: Demand Generation Surge
│   ├── Launch targeted campaigns
│   ├── Increase SDR activity targets
│   ├── Deploy referral programs
│   └── Activate partner channels
├── Week 5: Process Reinforcement
│   ├── Daily pipeline standups
│   ├── Weekly deep-dive reviews
│   ├── Manager coaching intensification
│   └── CRM hygiene enforcement
└── Week 6: Performance Monitoring
    ├── Track new pipeline creation
    ├── Monitor stage progression
    ├── Measure win rate recovery
    └── Adjust tactics based on data

PHASE 4: OPTIMIZE (Week 7-12)
├── Institutionalize new processes
├── Document lessons learned
├── Update playbooks
└── Transition to steady-state management
```
