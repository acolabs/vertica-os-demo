### PURPOSE

This document establishes the complete operating rhythm for inside sales organizations across the Vertica portfolio. It defines the cadence, structure, and accountability mechanisms that create predictable revenue outcomes and operational excellence.

**Core Philosophy:** *"The sales org sets the rhythm for the entire company. Up-and-to-the-right isn't just a metric—it's a cultural norm."*

## SECTION 2: DAILY RHYTHM

### 2.1 Daily Huddle (15 minutes)

**Frequency:** Every business day  
**Duration:** 15 minutes (hard stop)  
**Attendees:** Sales Manager + Team (AEs, SDRs)  
**Format:** Stand-up (virtual or in-person)

#### Agenda Template

| Time | Topic | Owner | Output |
|------|-------|-------|--------|
| 0:00-0:03 | Wins Celebration | Rotating | Recognition |
| 0:03-0:08 | Pipeline Blockers | Each AE | Action items |
| 0:08-0:13 | Today's Commitments | Each person | Accountability |
| 0:13-0:15 | Manager Priorities | Sales Mgr | Focus areas |

#### Huddle Questions (Each Person Answers)

1. **Yesterday:** What did you accomplish? (1 win)
2. **Today:** What are your top 3 priorities?
3. **Blockers:** What do you need help with?

## SECTION 3: WEEKLY RHYTHM

### 3.1 Weekly Pipeline Review (60 minutes)

**Frequency:** Weekly (Tuesday or Wednesday)  
**Duration:** 60 minutes  
**Attendees:** CRO_X, Sales Managers, AEs  
**Format:** Deal-by-deal inspection

#### Agenda Template

| Time | Topic | Owner | Materials |
|------|-------|-------|-----------|
| 0:00-0:05 | Week-in-Review Metrics | RevOps | Dashboard |
| 0:05-0:45 | Deal Inspection (3-4 deals/AE) | AEs | Opportunity notes |
| 0:45-0:55 | Pipeline Health Review | CRO_X | Stage analysis |
| 0:55-1:00 | Action Items & Next Steps | All | Action log |

#### Deal Inspection Framework (MEDDPICC Lite)

For each deal reviewed, AE must answer:

| Element | Question | Evidence Required |
|---------|----------|-------------------|
| **M**etrics | What business outcomes will this drive? | Quantified ROI |
| **E**conomic Buyer | Who can sign the check? | Contact confirmed |
| **D**ecision Criteria | How will they decide? | Documented requirements |
| **D**ecision Process | What are the steps to signature? | Timeline with owners |
| **P**aper Process | What procurement hoops exist? | Vendor setup status |
| **I**mplicate Pain | Why must they act NOW? | Urgency evidence |
| **C**hampion | Who's fighting for us internally? | Relationship strength (1-5) |
| **C**ompetition | Who else are they considering? | Competitive positioning |
