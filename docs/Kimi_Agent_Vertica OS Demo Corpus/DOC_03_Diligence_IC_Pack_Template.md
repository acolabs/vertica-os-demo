## 1. PURPOSE

This template provides the standardized structure for Investment Committee (IC) packs at Vertica Capital Partners. It ensures:
- **Consistency**: All IC materials follow the same format
- **Completeness**: No critical element is omitted
- **Evidence-Backing**: All claims supported by data and sources
- **Decision Clarity**: Clear recommendation with risk-adjusted rationale

**Primary Objective**: Enable IC members to make informed investment decisions with full context in a standardized, efficient format.

## 5. STEP-BY-STEP SOP: IC PACK PREPARATION

### PHASE 1: DILIGENCE KICKOFF (T+0 to T+3 Days)

#### Step 1.1: Diligence Planning

**Owner**: Principal

**Actions**:
1. Convene diligence kickoff meeting
2. Assign workstream owners
3. Set data room access and permissions
4. Establish diligence timeline (target: 4-6 weeks)
5. Create diligence tracker (shared)

**Diligence Tracker Template**:
```
| Workstream | Owner | Status | Target Date | Blockers |
|------------|-------|--------|-------------|----------|
| Financial DD | [FIRM_A] | In Progress | [DATE] | [NONE] |
| Commercial DD | PRINCIPAL_B | In Progress | [DATE] | [CUSTOMER_ACCESS] |
| Legal DD | [FIRM_B] | Not Started | [DATE] | [NDA] |
| ... | ... | ... | ... | ... |
```

#### Step 1.2: Data Room Setup

**Owner**: Associate

**Actions**:
1. Create data room structure (see Section 3.2)
2. Grant access to deal team and advisors
3. Upload initial documents received
4. Set up document request tracker
5. Establish version control protocol

---

### PHASE 2: DILIGENCE EXECUTION (T+3 to T+30 Days)

#### Step 2.1: Financial Diligence

**Key Areas**:
- Revenue recognition and quality
- Customer concentration and churn
- Gross margin sustainability
- Operating expense trends
- Working capital requirements
- Cash flow conversion

**Evidence Required**:
- [ ] Monthly financials (24 months)
- [ ] Customer-level revenue detail
- [ ] Contract review (top 20 customers)
- [ ] Audit workpapers (if available)

#### Step 2.2: Commercial Diligence

**Key Areas**:
- Market size and growth
- Competitive positioning
- Customer satisfaction and retention
- Pricing power
- Sales efficiency
- Product-market fit

**Evidence Required**:
- [ ] Customer reference calls (8-12)
- [ ] Win/loss analysis
- [ ] Net Promoter Score data
- [ ] Market research reports

**Customer Call Script**:
```
VERTICA CUSTOMER REFERENCE CALL GUIDE
Duration: 30 minutes
Attendees: PRINCIPAL_B, ASSOCIATE_C

1. CONTEXT (2 min)
   • Thank you for your time
   • Vertica diligence on [Company]
   • Confidentiality assurance

2. RELATIONSHIP (3 min)
   • How long have you been a customer?
   • What was the decision process to select [Company]?
   • What alternatives did you consider?

3. PRODUCT/VALUE (8 min)
   • What problem does [Company] solve for you?
   • How critical is it to your operations?
   • What quantifiable value have you received?
   • What would happen if you stopped using it?

4. SATISFACTION (7 min)
   • How satisfied are you with the product? (1-10)
   • How satisfied with support/service? (1-10)
   • Any major issues or complaints?
   • What would you improve?

5. RETENTION/EXPANSION (5 min)
   • Any plans to expand usage?
   • Any plans to reduce or cancel?
   • What would cause you to leave?
   • How does pricing compare to alternatives?

6. MARKET PERSPECTIVE (5 min)
   • How do you see [Company] vs. competitors?
   • What trends do you see in this space?
   • Would you recommend [Company] to peers?

CLOSING
• Thank you for your candor
• May we follow up if needed?
```

### PHASE 3: IC PACK ASSEMBLY (T+25 to T+35 Days)

#### Step 3.1: Draft Executive Summary

**Template**: See Section 8.1

**Key Elements**:
1. Recommendation (Approve/Reject/Conditional)
2. Investment highlights
3. Key risks and mitigants
4. Financial summary
5. Timeline to close

#### Step 3.2: Finalize Financial Model

**Required Outputs**:
- [ ] Base case, upside, downside scenarios
- [ ] Sensitivity analysis (key variables)
- [ ] Returns analysis (IRR, MOIC)
- [ ] Sources and uses
- [ ] Pro forma capitalization

#### Step 3.3: Complete Risk Register

**Template**: See Section 8.4

**Requirements**:
- Minimum 10 identified risks
- Each risk rated (Probability x Impact)
- Mitigation strategy for each
- Residual risk assessment

#### Step 3.4: Finalize Value Creation Plan

**Owner**: Operating Partner

**Requirements**:
- 100-day plan (first 3 months)
- Year 1 priorities
- Year 2-3 strategic initiatives
- Resource requirements
- Success metrics

#### Step 3.5: Compile Appendices

**Required Appendices**:
- [ ] Detailed financial model
- [ ] Customer call summaries
- [ ] Diligence reports (external)
- [ ] Management bios
- [ ] Market research
- [ ] Comparable company analysis

---

### PHASE 4: IC MEETING (T+35 to T+40 Days)

#### Step 4.1: Pre-IC Review

**Owner**: Managing Partner Sponsor

**Actions**:
1. Review complete IC pack
2. Identify open questions
3. Prepare for IC discussion
4. Confirm IC attendance

#### Step 4.2: IC Presentation

**Format**: 60-minute meeting
**Agenda**:
```
1. Executive Summary (10 min) - Principal
2. Investment Thesis (10 min) - Principal
3. Diligence Findings (15 min) - Deal team
4. Value Creation Plan (10 min) - Operating Partner
5. Risk Discussion (10 min) - All
6. Deliberation & Vote (5 min) - Managing Partners
```

#### Step 4.3: IC Vote

**Voting Options**:
- **Approve**: Proceed to close
- **Approve with Conditions**: Proceed subject to specified conditions
- **Reject**: Do not proceed
- **Defer**: Additional information required

**Conditions Precedent** (if applicable):
- [ ] Specific issue resolution
- [ ] Additional diligence
- [ ] Documentation changes
- [ ] Third-party approvals

## 7. FAILURE MODES

### 7.1 Diligence Failures

| Failure Mode | Symptom | Mitigation |
|--------------|---------|------------|
| **Scope Creep** | Diligence extends >8 weeks | Fixed timeline, weekly check-ins |
| **Customer Access Issues** | <6 reference calls | Contractual access requirement |
| **Data Room Gaps** | Key documents missing | Standardized request list |
| **Advisor Overrun** | Costs exceed budget | Fixed-fee arrangements |
| **Surprise Issues** | Material finding post-IC | Red team review before IC |

### 7.2 IC Failures

| Failure Mode | Symptom | Mitigation |
|--------------|---------|------------|
| **Incomplete Pack** | Missing sections | Checklist validation |
| **Late Distribution** | <48 hours before IC | Production timeline |
| **Unclear Recommendation** | IC confused on position | Explicit recommendation section |
| **Insufficient Risk Disclosure** | Post-close surprises | Independent risk review |
| **Value Creation Gaps** | VCP not credible | OP sign-off required |
